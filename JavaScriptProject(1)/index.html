Hotels and Directions

Hospitality Challets

Get a front-row seat to the action. Tickets for our hospitality challets give you access to a private viewing area with air conditioned tents, food and private bathrooms.

Landing Zone

Experience “The Greatest Show Above the Earth”

The Air and Sea show will showcase the men, women, technology and equipment from all five branches of the United States military as well as our police, firefighters and first responder agencies.

This two-day spectacular event will feature civilian aerobatic acts, offshore powerboat racing demonstrations, extreme water sports and more, all on the beautiful shores of Miami Beach.

Back

Display Village

Visit the Display Village, which spans more than 6 football fields, and includes interactive displays from all branches of the military, as well as an obstacle course, corporate sponsors, food and beverage tents.

Displays also include event memorabilia and merchandise with a portion of profits given to charity

Back

Music Explosion

As the jets fly overhead, you will be entertained by some of America’s top musical acts playing live on the National Salute to America’s Heroes stage at the center of the Air and Sea Show.

The Music Explosion will feature a line up of today’s hottest artists. These artists will perform a musical tribute to America’s heroes to be broadcast as a national primetime television event.
