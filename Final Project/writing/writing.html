Read Write Learning Center

Teacher Biographies for Website:

​

Julie Rentz founded Read-Write: The Learning Center in 2011. She has over 25 years of experience working with children with learning differences. Julie serves as a member of the Board of Directors for the Alabama Branch of the International Dyslexia Association. Julie was named an “Unsung Hero” in the International Dyslexia Association’s “Honor a Teacher Campaign.” Additionally, she is the founder of the Mobile Bay group of the Alabama International Dyslexia Association. She is also a member of the Learning Disability Association and has made presentations on dyslexia for local groups, including schools and the Bay Area Speech and Hearing Association.

LEEP Public Relations Plan:

​

Challah for Hunger: Class Assignment

Backgrounder for Challah for Hunger:

​

Challah for Hunger was established at University of Miami in 2010. The chapter became a national affiliate for Challah for Hunger in 2015, working with Challah for Hunger’s national organization and donating a portion of its profits to Challah for Hunger’s national philanthropy.

The student-run organization raises awareness and funds to fight hunger in South Florida and throughout the world by baking and selling challah bread. It also promotes building a community of students at the University of Miami by involving students, both Jewish and not Jewish, in an aspect of Jewish culture. 200 students are members of the organization’s Facebook page for volunteers, and around 40 students are actively involved in the organization each semester.
