Hello,

I'm Mary Bryant, a senior at the University of Miami majoring in Public Relations with minors in Marketing and Psychology. Growing up in Alabama I didn't know much abut Public Relations, but through my classes and my involvement in student organizations at the University of Miami, I discovered my love for PR and developed important communication skills.

My involvement with the University of Miami’s Public Relations Experience Program allowed

me to work at events in the Miami area including the WGC Cadillac Championship and

NASCAR Championship Weekend. These experiences required me to communicate

important information to guests and quickly resolve any issues that arose

with media registration.I also gained experience with the University of

Miami President’s 100. Leading admissions tours for the President's 100 requires strong

interpersonal skills, flexibility and the ability to maintain a positive attitude as I am tasked

with persuading students to attend the University and responding to the concerns of their

family members.

Outside of class, I love yoga and scuba diving with the University of Miami Scuba Club.

In addition to my experience, I am an advanced writer. I also have experience with Photoshop,

InDesign and Illustrator, and am able to grasp software quickly.
